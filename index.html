About this assessment

You'll work through a real sales conversation with a new MINT member who has never heard of Red Light Therapy. The conversation unfolds in four stages — each time you submit a video, the member responds and the next stage unlocks.

1The opening — greet the member and introduce RLT
2The pitch — explain the science and benefits
3The objection — handle the member's concern about value
4The close — book the session and seal the deal
Each stage unlocks only after the previous video is submitted — take your time with each one.
